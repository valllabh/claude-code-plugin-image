## findings

- Cache savings on paraphrased intents are real: ~30% token reduction and zero model image processing on warm hits.
- Worker followed "no preamble" rule cleanly across all five runs after the rule was added.
- Worker chose `shasum -a 256` fallback even when python3 was available. Minor: not wrong, just not preferred. Worth tightening the prompt to prefer the bundled helper first.
